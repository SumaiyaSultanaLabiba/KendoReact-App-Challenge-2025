# Surah Al-Inshiqaq
Surah: Al-Inshiqaq | Chapter: 84

84|1|When the heaven is split asunder,
84|2|And listens to and obeys its Lord -- and it must do so.
84|3|And when the earth is stretched forth,
84|4|And has cast out all that was in it and became empty.
84|5|And listens to and obeys its Lord -- and it must do so.
84|6|O man! Verily, you are returning towards your Lord with your deeds and actions, a sure returning, and you will meet.
84|7|Then as for him who will be given his Record in his right hand,
84|8|He surely will receive an easy reckoning,
84|9|And will return to his family Masrur (in joy)!
84|10|But whosoever is given his Record behind his back,
84|11|He will invoke destruction,
84|12|And he shall enter a blazing Fire, and made to taste its burning.
84|13|Verily, he was among his people in joy!
84|14|Verily, he thought that he would never return!
84|15|Yes! Verily, his Lord has been ever beholding him!
84|16|But no! I swear by Ash-Shafaq;
84|17|And the night and what it Wasaqa,
84|18|And the moon when it Ittasaq.
84|19|You shall certainly travel from stage
84|20|What is the matter with them, that they believe not
84|21|And when the Qur'an is recited to them, they fall not prostrate.
84|22|Nay, those who disbelieve deny.
84|23|And Allah knows best what they gather,
84|24|So, announce to them a painful torment.
