# Surah Al-Inshirah
Surah: Al-Inshirah | Chapter: 94

94|1|Have We not opened your breast for you
94|2|And removed from you your burden.
94|3|Which weighed down your back
94|4|And have We not raised high your fame
94|5|Verily, along with every hardship is relief,
94|6|Verily, along with every hardship is relief.
94|7|So when you have finished, devote yourself to Allah's worship.
94|8|And to your Lord turn intentions and hopes.
